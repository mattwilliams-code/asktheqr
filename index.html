You choose what's on the page.
There is nothing else on it.

A QR code on a child's coat makes people uneasy, and it should. So the page is built the boring way: it's a list of the fields you filled in, and that's the whole thing.

  • Every field except the name is optional — leave it blank and it isn't on the page
  • No account to make, no app to install, for you or for whoever finds it
  • No location, no tracking, no scan history — the page can't know where it was scanned
  • You see the exact page before you pay
